About Me

Hi, I’m Tyheim Martin, a graphic designer, illustrator, and animator based in New York. I specialize in bold, culturally inspired visual storytelling through digital and traditional media. My work often explores identity, motion, and emotion. I’m currently studying Communication Design at City Tech.
